About MindWell

MindWell is a comprehensive mental health web application designed to help you understand, track, and improve your mental wellbeing. Whether you're looking to monitor your mood, assess your mental health, or find resources for coping strategies, MindWell offers tools to support your journey.

Mental health is just as important as physical health, and we believe in making mental wellness accessible to everyone. Our application provides evidence-based assessments, mood tracking, and educational resources to empower you in taking control of your mental wellbeing.
